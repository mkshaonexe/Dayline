# Privacy Policy for Dayline

**Last updated:** December 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Dayline ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and related services. Including the Dayline Web App and Desktop integration.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our Service to you.

### A. Personal Data
When you create an account to use our synchronization features (Cloud Sync), we may collect personally identifiable information, such as:
*   **Email address** (via Google Sign-In or manual registration).
*   **Profile information** (Name, Avatar).

### B. User Content (App Data)
To provide the core functionality of Dayline (daily planning, journaling, task management), we process the data you input into the App:
*   **Tasks and To-Do Lists**: Titles, descriptions, dates, and completion status.
*   **Journals**: Text entries and associated metadata.
*   **Settings**: Your theme preferences, notification settings, and alarm configurations.

**Note:** This data is uploaded to our secure servers (powered by Supabase) **solely for the purpose of synchronizing your data** across your devices (Android, Web, Desktop). We do not sell or analyze the content of your private tasks or journals for advertising purposes.

### C. Usage Data & Analytics (Firebase)
We use usage data to understand how the App is used and to improve the user experience. This data is collected automatically and may include:
*   Device Internet Protocol (IP) address.
*   Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
*   Pages visited, time spent on pages, and interaction patterns (e.g., "Add Task" button clicks).
*   **Firebase Analytics**: We use Google Firebase Analytics to aggregate this anonymous usage data.

## 2. Third-Party Services

We employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our App ("Service Providers"), to provide the Service on our behalf, or to assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

### A. Google Firebase
We use multiple Firebase services to enhance the App:
*   **Firebase Analytics**: To track app usage and stability.
*   **Firebase Crashlytics**: To collect crash reports and logs when the App malfunctions. This helps us fix bugs quickly.
*   **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)**: To send you push notifications, including reminders for your tasks and important app updates.

For more information on the privacy practices of Google, please visit the [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy) web page.

### B. Supabase
We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider.
*   **Data Storage**: Your user content (Tasks, Journals) is stored securely in Supabase databases.
*   **Authentication**: Supabase manages user authentication and security.

For more information, visit the [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

## 3. Permissions

The App requires certain permissions to function effectively:
*   **Notifications**: To send you reminders and alerts.
*   **Alarms & Reminders (Schedule Exact Alarm)**: To ring alarms at the precise times you set for your daily tasks.
*   **Background Usage**: To ensure the App can sync data and trigger alarms even when not actively open.
*   **Overlay**: To display "Wake Up" screens or critical alerts over other apps.

## 4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) when transmitting data between your device and our servers. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 5. Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data.
*   **In-App**: You can request account deletion via the "Settings" > "Profile" > "Delete Account" option (if available).
*   **Contact**: You may also contact us at [support email] to request data deletion. Upon request, we will permanently remove all your Personal Data and User Content from our servers.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
